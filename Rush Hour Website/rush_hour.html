Project Summary

RUSH HOUR is a traffic management project, incorporating the fields of Machine Learning, Web Development and App Development. At each traffic signal, for each side, it counts the number of cars, scooters, buses, etc., and then assigns a corresponding priority and time for that particular signal, instead of just assigning a fixed time to all irrespective of the number of vehicles present at that time. Thus, this will save time of the commuters. Also, it contains a special feature for ambulances, fire-fighters and other emergency-response teams to mark in their location of their starting and destination points and according to it, algorithms of all the traffic signals along that way will be altered in a certain way, allowing the emergency vehicles to pass the signal without any stopping at the signal.
